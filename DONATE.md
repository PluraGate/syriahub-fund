# Donate

> How to support SyriaHub development.

---

## Primary: GitHub Sponsors

**[Sponsor PluraGate on GitHub](https://github.com/sponsors/PluraGate)**

GitHub Sponsors is our preferred funding channel because:
- Zero platform fees (GitHub covers them)
- Direct integration with our development workflow
- Transparent contribution history
- Eligible for GitHub Fund matching

### Sponsorship Tiers

| Tier | Monthly | What It Funds |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **Supporter** | $5 | A cup of coffee + our gratitude |
| **Researcher** | $25 | 1 hour of development time |
| **Contributor** | $100 | Bug fixes + maintenance for a week |
| **Sustainer** | $500 | Infrastructure costs for a month |
| **Partner** | $1,000+ | Feature development capacity |

## Tax Considerations

PluraGate is not currently a registered charity. Donations may not be tax-deductible depending on your jurisdiction.

We are exploring fiscal sponsorship options for tax-advantaged giving in the US, UK, and EU.

## Recognition

**All sponsors** (at any level) receive:
- Name in SPONSORS.md (unless anonymous)
- Quarterly impact updates
- Early access to roadmap discussions

**Partner-level sponsors** ($1,000+/mo) additionally receive:
- Logo on README (optional)
- Direct communication channel
- Input on feature prioritisation

## FAQ

**Q: Is my donation tax-deductible?**
A: Currently, no. We're exploring fiscal sponsorship options.

**Q: Can I donate anonymously?**
A: Yes. GitHub Sponsors supports private sponsorships.

**Q: Can my company sponsor?**
A: Absolutely. Contact us for invoicing and partnership options.

**Q: What if I can't donate money?**
A: Contributions of code, translations, or documentation are equally valuable. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/PluraGate/SyriaHub/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).

**Q: How do I know my money is used well?**
A: We publish quarterly spend reports and an annual financial summary. See [BUDGET.md](BUDGET.md).
